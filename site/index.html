Built for how the work actually goes.

Every step happens on the canvas — grounded on what you have already made, and streamed in as cards you can keep shaping.

Skip the blank canvas

Hand Jarwiz a goal — “plan my Goa weekend,” “size this market” — and Autopilot does the first draft for you: it plans, researches the live web, and builds the whole board, card by card, while you watch. You start from something, never nothing.

Stay in your flow

Ask in the prompt bar and the answer streams onto a card, right where you are. Select a card first and the question grounds on exactly what is in front of you — no context to re-explain.

Trust what it tells you

Research-heavy asks pull live data from the web and cite their sources — grounded in what is true today, not what a model happens to remember. You can check the receipts.

Start from what you have

Drop a PDF, spreadsheet, video, or link onto the canvas and Jarwiz reads it — transcripts, tables, and key frames — then works from your material instead of a blank page.

Change your mind, cheaply

Make it shorter, go deeper, turn a doc into a table or a flowchart, or regenerate — one bar, right above the card. Reworking an idea costs a click, not a restart.

Find the signal in the mess

Collapse a wall of sticky notes into named themes in a single click. Jarwiz also watches the board on its own — flagging tensions and gaps between your cards before you would have spotted them.
