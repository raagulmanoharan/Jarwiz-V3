Capabilities

An AI collaborator, not a chatbot in a sidebar.

Everything happens on the canvas — grounded on what you've already made, and streamed in as cards you can keep working.

Autopilot

Hand Jarwiz a goal in the prompt bar — "plan my Goa weekend", "size this market" — and it plans, researches the live web, and builds the whole board for you, card by card, while you watch.

Ask & create

Type a prompt and the answer streams onto a card. Select a card first to ground the question on exactly what's in front of you.

Deep research, cited

Research-heavy work pulls live data from the web and cites its sources — grounded in what's true today, not what the model remembers.

Bring your material

Drop a PDF, spreadsheet, video or link onto the canvas. Jarwiz reads it — transcripts, tables, key frames — and works from it.

Reshape any card

Make it shorter, go deeper, turn a doc into a table or a flowchart, or regenerate — one bar, right above the card.

Cluster, summarise & proactive comments

Turn a wall of sticky notes into named themes in one click — and let Jarwiz notice tensions or gaps between your cards and leave FigJam-style comments, unprompted.

How it works

Three moves from blank to finished.

Drop, ask, or run

Drop in your material, ask a question in the prompt bar, or pull a Thinking Machine off the left rail and type a subject.

Jarwiz researches & builds

It searches the live web, reasons through a real framework, and lays the result out as a board of cards — right where you're working.

Refine on the canvas

Move, connect, reshape and deepen the cards. Every artifact stays live — argue with it, extend it, or hand it off.
